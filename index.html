Hello, We're ENCOUNTER

We are a campus based direct food delivery service. Buildt/Ran by students for students, staff, and members of the University. There's no denying that the campus of University at Albany is fairly large. And for students who dorm further away from the campus center/ dining halls, getting food is a hasel. And even with conventional food delivery services such as dominos, Uber, Grubhub, postmates etc. Getting delivery is not a piece of cake. This is an architectural demosite/ trail run for campus based delivery service in just 5 webpages.
Not only do hungry students get adequate service, but students in need of employment can have the opportunity to work with flexibility. The goal of the site is to provide users with safe delivery services on demand options.
